Bitcoin Price Breaks $400

Bitcoin, the world's most popular digital currency (cryptocurrency) has finally went past the $400 USD mark for the first time ever since last year. (November 2014) After a price dip well below $200 USD in January, Bitcoin traded at the top of $410 USD Tueday afternoon. However, Bitcoin's price today still marks a strong contrast to its value back in November 2013, when the price peaked at an all-time of $1,200 USD.

No one the real answer as to why the #1 digital currency is increasing exponentally in value. Some users on Reddit (/r/bitcoin) are saying that demand is mostly been coming from China, since the daily volume of Bitcoin trades from China has been 2x - 3x the normal amount over the past 2'ish weeks. In addition, the widespread use of Bitcoin has been steadily increasing as more and more people are aware of it. Therefore, increasing the value of Bitcoin. The number of daily Bitcoin transactions appears to be increasing a lot during the past several days, according to the popular Bitcoin wallet site, Coinbase.
